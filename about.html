About Us
Senior Infocomm Club is an interest driven club in River Valley Valley School focused on exploring modern technologies and theirs applications. We have great emphasis on the holistic development of our members. Thus, being in Senior Infocomm Club allows learning of 21st century information technology skills as well as virtues such as unending curiousity for knowledges and perseverence when met with challenges.

We do intensive research in the technological fields such as Artificial Intelligence, Drone Programming, Web Application Development, Game Development and many others. We also have an extremely competitive mindset and regularly participate in computing related competitions such as the National Olympiad of Informations, National Coding Championship and Hackathons.
Our Motto
Innovation from the Heart
In Senior Infocomm, we are constantly challenging ourselves by coming up with innovative solutions to solve problems and invent ways to benefit the school and the community. We aim to improves the lives of the people around us through intelligent applications of our technical skills and expertises.
Passion at its Core
As an interest-driven CCA, we have an unwavering passion and drive to pursue our interests and have what it takes to perservere through challenges. We regularly stay up to date with cutting-edge technologies and equip ourselves with the necessary technical skills to overcome potential problems.
