PROJECTS
  River Valley Senior Infocomm Club follows a project based structure that allows the members to choose to work in the areas that they are interested in. We do intensive research in the technological fields such as Artificial Intelligence, Drone Programming, Web Application Development, Game Development and many other areas.

PYTHON

  Python is an interpreted, high-level, general-purpose programming language. It is a beginner friendly programming language that allows high readability of code and provides constructs that enable clear programming on both small and large scales. We employ Python as a scripting language for back-end web development, designing interesting games as well as being the primary language for developing Artificial Intelligence.

WEB APPLICATION DEVELOPMENT

  Senior Infocomm Club develop web applications that better the community through greater convenience. Applications such as automated timetable and seating arrangement generator reduces the work needing for mundane processes. Furthermore, we also designs websites for publicity for organisations and events, such as this very website.

DRONE PROGRAMMING

  Artificial Intelligence is a technology with increasing popularity and influence as students who are interested in the area of engineering spend time to research and programming the drones to complete fun task such as target pursue or obstacle course.

ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

  Artificial Intelligence is also a technology with increasing popularity and influence due to its applications in many areas and industries. Hence, we have great interest in the topic and actively conduct research by participating in AI related competitions.
