WORKSHOPS
  River Valley Senior Infocomm Club frequently organises workshop for participants within the school. The aim is to improve computing literacy and competencies not just for the members of Infocomm Club but also for the rest of the school. We aim to not just teach the participants, but also inspire them to further explore areas of computing either as a hobby or a practical skill to have. Through learning of the basics of programming, we hope to help participants overcome the beginner barrier and allow them to recognise their talents in the field.
